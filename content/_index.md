## Introduction

Hi, I’m Peter Verheijen, and welcome to my personal website.

I currently work as an Engineering Manager at Straumann, where I focus on building and scaling Data, MLOps, and Machine Learning Platform teams and infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of engineering leadership, machine learning operations, cloud platforms, and organizational design.

I’m passionate about creating high-performing engineering environments where people can collaborate effectively, challenge ideas openly, and continue growing together. My leadership style combines strategic thinking with a hands-on technical background, shaped by years of experience in software engineering, machine learning, and platform development.

Colleagues often describe me as enthusiastic, communicative, and people-oriented. I care deeply about clarity, ownership, and helping teams navigate complexity while still delivering meaningful results.

On this website, I share articles about engineering leadership, MLOps, platform engineering, software architecture, organizational scaling, and lessons learned from working in AI-driven environments.

I also write about Rust from the perspective of a Python developer. If you’re a Pythonista interested in learning Rust, you can follow my journey and tutorials at [pythontorust.nl](https://pythontorust.nl).

Feel free to explore the articles and subscribe if you'd like to stay updated on new posts and projects.
